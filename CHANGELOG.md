# What's New in Embara

A plain-language history of changes. For the latest version's store text, see the
"What's new" section on Google Play.

## 1.2.1
- **You stay signed in.** Fixed an issue where Embara could sign you out and make
  you log in again almost every time you reopened it. Your session now sticks
  reliably — even when your phone closes the app in the background.

## 1.2.0
- Refreshed app icon and branding.
- Fixed a layout glitch where a panel could get cut off on landscape tablets.
- Smaller app download.

## 1.1.1
- Fixed pull-to-refresh not working on some screens.

## 1.1.0
- Cleaner navigation — a native bar replaces the old pop-up overlay.
- Pull-to-refresh improvements.

## 1.0.2
- Security and privacy hardening for safer connections.

## 1.0.0
- First release of Embara.
